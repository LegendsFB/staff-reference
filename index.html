Game Day Timeline

−2h / −1.5h
Clubs open. Starts slow — depends on giveaway, day/night, day of week. KPMG fills first.
−45 min
Big push — majority of guests arrive. First drink + food to seat.
Anthems
Stop all in-seat service during national anthems.
Inn. 1–3
Big rush. 1st & 2nd drinks, main food orders. Learn your guests — LEAP into action.
Mid 7th
Snack walls & non-alcoholic fridges close. Bottled water available at bar on request.
Bot. 8th
⚠️ FINAL LAST CALL — in-seat and club bar.
9th Inn.
No in-seat service in the bowl.
Post-game
Bars in club remain open 30 minutes post-game with in-club entertainment.

Acceptable IDs — Always ID Under 40

✓ Valid provincial driver's licence or out-of-province photo driver's licence
✓ Canadian Armed Forces Identification Card
✓ Valid Passport
✓ Certificate of Indian Status
✓ Canadian Citizenship Card
✓ Permanent Resident Card
✓ BYID card issued by the Liquor Control Board of Ontario
⚠️ Do not serve overly intoxicated individuals. When in doubt — do not serve.
⚠️ Do not serve drinks ordered for someone who is not yet present.
💬 Keep it simple: "As a matter of policy, I'm not able to serve any more alcohol."
